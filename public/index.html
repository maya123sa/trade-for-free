üí° Trading Tricks & Tips

üéØ Support & Resistance Trick

Look for at least 3 touches on a level before considering it strong support/resistance. Use previous day's high/low as intraday levels.

üìà Volume Confirmation

Always confirm breakouts with volume. A breakout with 2x average volume is more reliable than one with low volume.

‚è∞ Time-Based Analysis

First 30 minutes (9:15-9:45 AM) often set the tone. Watch for ORB setups. Avoid trading during 11:30-1:30 PM (lunch time volatility).

üîÑ Multiple Timeframe Confirmation

Use higher timeframe for trend direction and lower timeframe for entry. Daily for trend, hourly for entry timing.

üí∞ Risk Management

Never risk more than 2% of capital per trade. Use 1:2 risk-reward ratio minimum. Set stop-loss before entering trade.

üìä Indicator Combinations

RSI + MACD for momentum, Bollinger Bands + RSI for mean reversion, SuperTrend + EMA for trend following.

üéØ Gap Trading Strategy

Gap up > 2%: Wait for pullback to VWAP before buying. Gap down > 2%: Look for bounce from previous day's low. Most gaps fill within 2-3 days.

üìä Pivot Point Strategy

Above PP = Bullish bias, target R1/R2. Below PP = Bearish bias, target S1/S2. Use PP as dynamic support/resistance during the day.

‚ö° Momentum Scalping

RSI > 60 + Price > VWAP + Volume > 1.5x avg = Bullish momentum. Exit when RSI hits 80 or price falls below VWAP.

üîÑ Mean Reversion Play

RSI < 30 + Price touching lower Bollinger Band + Hammer/Doji candle = Potential reversal. Target middle BB line.

üìà Trend Following Rules

SuperTrend green + EMA 20 > EMA 50 + ADX > 25 = Strong uptrend. Only take long positions. Exit when SuperTrend turns red.

üé™ Option Chain Insights

PCR > 1.5 = Bullish (more puts), PCR < 0.7 = Bearish (more calls). Max Pain acts as magnet. High OI strikes act as support/resistance.

‚è∞ Intraday Time Cycles

9:15-10:00: Initial momentum. 10:00-11:30: Trend continuation. 11:30-1:30: Consolidation (avoid). 1:30-3:00: Afternoon trend. 3:00-3:30: Closing moves.

üéØ Fibonacci Retracement

In uptrend: Buy at 38.2% or 50% retracement. In downtrend: Sell at 38.2% or 50% bounce. 61.8% break = trend reversal.

üìä Volume Profile Trading

POC (Point of Control) acts as strong support/resistance. Price tends to return to POC. VAH/VAL are key levels for breakouts.

üöÄ Breakout Confirmation

Valid breakout needs: 1) Volume > 2x average, 2) Close above resistance, 3) No immediate pullback below breakout level, 4) Follow-through next day.

üí° News Trading Strategy

Buy the rumor, sell the news. Positive news + gap up = Often sells off. Negative news + gap down = Often bounces. Trade the opposite of obvious reaction.

üé™ Sector Rotation Play

When Nifty is flat, look for sector rotation. Banking up = Financials outperform. IT up = Export stocks strong. Auto up = Domestic consumption theme.
